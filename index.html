About the Event

The Hand Table Tennis World Cup 2023 is the Third Edition of the Cup, where 5 of the best teams in the World go head-to-head in pursuit of glory. The previous 2 editions saw an intense battle among member teams with 4 teams already being Finalists - this speaking a lot about the quality of the game played here.

The Champions of the previous editions are China (2021) and Great Britain (2022) respectively. The World's biggest sporting event is once again set to rock the stage this Winter 2023, to be hosted once again at the New Arena in China. To know who will be the best of the best - you must keep your eyes on this space for more info...
