About the Event

The Hand Table Tennis World Cup 2024 is the Third Edition of the Cup, where 5 of the best teams in the World go head-to-head in pursuit of glory. The previous 2 editions saw an intense battle among member teams with 4 teams already being Finalists - this speaking a lot about the quality of the game played here.

The Champions of the previous editions are China (2021) and Great Britain (2022) respectively. The World's biggest sporting event is once again set to rock the stage in 2024, to be hosted once at the New Arena. To know who will be the best of the best - you must keep your eyes on this space for more info...

Net Run Rate (NRR) is to be calculated as the Ratio of Margin of Victory in Points to The Total Number of Points played for in that game. It is to be added to the Team that emerges Victorious and subtracted from the Team that Loses. In case of a Tie in the Points table, the Tie Breaker would be on the Net Run Rate for a higher position.
